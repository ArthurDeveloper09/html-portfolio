The Best Action Movies According to Arthur

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Edge of Tommorow

The story revolves around Major William Cage (Tom Cruise) who is
caught in a time loop, reliving the same day over and over after dying
in a battle against an alien race. This premise creates a sense of urgency
and unpredictability, making you wonder how he’ll break free.

Fast and Furios

There’s a sense of awe and disbelief when you watch the insane car maneuvers,
whether it’s jumping between skyscrapers or driving on collapsing ice.
The physics may not always be realistic, but they are designed to be exhilarating

Hero Work Out

Hero workouts are often much longer and more difficult than typical
CrossFit WODs. They are meant to push you to your physical and mental
limits. This intensity reflects the hardships and sacrifices made by
the individuals they are named for.
